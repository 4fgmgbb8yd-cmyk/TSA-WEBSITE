About This Project

Preserving the Story of Our Community

This website was created to explore and preserve the history of our local area. By collecting information about important events, historical figures, and well-known landmarks, this project helps explain how our community developed over time.

Learning about local history helps us understand where we come from and why our town is the way it is today. This site is designed to inform, educate, and encourage appreciation for the past.
